The Labyrinth

You wake up in a Labyrinth of tunnels inhabited by beasts and monsters. Can you survive? How fast can you find the keys and reach the locked door?

Game Play

You walk through tunnels, feeling for walls, fighting monsters and searching for the key to unlock the escape door.

This game is played using a keyboard. The player can move in one of any 4 directions (WASD). The player can also swing their sword in any of the 4 directions (Up, Left, Down, Right)
Press R to restart the current level.

audio and text feedback is provided depending on where the player tried to move/attack.

A timer is displayed at the end of the game. High scores and a scoreboard should be developed soon
